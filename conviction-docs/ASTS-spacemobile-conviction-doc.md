## COMPANY OVERVIEW

**Founded:** 2017  
**HQ:** Midland, Texas  
**CEO:** Abel Avellan (founder, former Ericsson, UN Broadband Commission)  
**Employees:** 578  
**Market Cap:** $38.4B (Feb 2, 2026)  
**Public:** April 2021 via SPAC (New Providence)  

**Business Model:**  
Space-based cellular broadband network delivering 4G/5G directly to standard smartphones globally. No special hardware, no apps, no modifications. The SpaceMobile constellation uses massive BlueBird satellites (largest commercial communications arrays in LEO) to provide coverage in remote/underserved regions.

**Technology:**  
- **Block 1 BlueBirds:** 693 sq ft antenna arrays, 5 satellites launched Sept 2024  
- **Block 2 BlueBirds:** 2,400 sq ft arrays (3x larger), 10x data capacity, 120 Mbit/s speeds  
- **Orbital altitude:** 508-740 km (LEO)  
- **Planned constellation:** ~90 satellites for global coverage (vs thousands for Starlink)
