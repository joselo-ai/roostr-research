## THE SOLUTION

**18 AI agents hunt stocks daily** ‚Üí **Ensemble votes** ‚Üí **Human risk manager enforces discipline**

### How It Works:

1. **Agents scan 100+ stocks daily** (Social Arbitrage, Value, Catalyst, Buffett, Munger, Burry, etc.)
2. **Agents suggest top 5-10** (highest conviction opportunities)
3. **Agents debate ‚Üí vote ‚Üí conviction score** (0-10)
4. **Joselo validates** (stops, sizing, risk caps)
5. **Deploy if approved** (>7/10 conviction + risk pass)
6. **Publish publicly** (GitHub, dashboard, Twitter)

### The Edge:

- **Scale:** 100+ stocks/day (vs 1-2 manually)
- **Systematic:** No emotion, no FOMO, no fear
- **Diverse:** 18 perspectives > 1
- **Disciplined:** -8% to -10% stops (enforced, no exceptions)
- **Transparent:** Every decision public (GitHub, dashboard)
