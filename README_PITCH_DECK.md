## ðŸ’¡ Pro Tips from the Field

### What Works
- **Show, don't tell:** Live demo of AI agents beats any slide
- **Proof of execution:** "Built in 2 hours" is the best credibility signal
- **Transparent track record:** Public dashboard builds trust (investors hate black boxes)
- **Specific numbers:** "65-110% returns" is more credible than "high returns"

### What Doesn't Work
- **Hype language:** "Revolutionary," "game-changing," "disrupting" (investors hate buzzwords)
- **Vague claims:** "We use AI" (everyone says that â€” show the architecture)
- **Backtests only:** "We simulated 100% returns" (means nothing without live trades)
- **Stealth mode:** "We'll share details after NDA" (red flag for investors)

- **"Why hasn't this been done before?"** â†’ Claude Sonnet 4 launched Jan 2025, this wasn't possible 12 months ago
