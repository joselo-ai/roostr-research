## Design Philosophy

**Minimal but Impactful**
- Clean, spacious layouts (plenty of white space)
- Bold typography (48-72pt headlines)
- Limited color palette (black, white, green accent)
- Data-driven visuals (charts > decorative elements)

**Professional but Modern**
- Not corporate boring (avoid blue/gray traditional VC decks)
- Not crypto flashy (avoid gradients, 3D, over-animation)
- roostr aesthetic: Technical, confident, slightly edgy

**Optimized for Remote Viewing**
- Readable at small sizes (Zoom calls, laptop screens)
- High contrast (works on any display)
- Minimal text per slide (visuals tell the story)
- Fast cognitive processing (investors scan in 3 seconds)

## Brand Guidelines

### Colors

| Color | Hex | Usage |
|-------|-----|-------|
| **roostr black** | `#000000` | Headlines, primary text, data labels |
| **roostr white** | `#FFFFFF` | Backgrounds, negative space |
| **roostr green** | `#4ade80` | Accent, CTAs, positive metrics, growth |
| **deep black** | `#0a0a0a` | Secondary backgrounds, depth |
| **light gray** | `#e0e0e0` | Secondary text, captions |

### Typography

| Element | Font | Size | Weight | Color |
|---------|------|------|--------|-------|
| **Slide Title** | Inter | 72pt | Bold (700) | Black |
| **Section Header** | Inter | 48pt | Bold (700) | Black |
| **Body Text** | Inter | 24pt | Regular (400) | Black |
| **Hero Number** | Inter | 96pt | Bold (700) | Green |
| **Data Label** | JetBrains Mono | 20pt | Medium (500) | Black |
| **Caption** | Inter | 18pt | Regular (400) | Light Gray |

### Spacing

- **Base unit:** 8px (all spacing in multiples of 8)
- **Safe zone:** 120px left/right margins, 80px top/bottom
- **Grid:** 12 columns, 20px gutters
- **Vertical rhythm:** 16px, 24px, 32px, 48px, 64px, 96px
