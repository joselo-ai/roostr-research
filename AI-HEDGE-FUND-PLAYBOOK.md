### **Risk Management Rules (Non-Negotiable)**

1. **Never risk more than 2% of portfolio on single trade**
   - Conviction plays: 1% risk (tight stops)
   - Moonshots: 2% risk (wider stops, smaller size)

2. **Position sizing formula:**
   - Risk Amount = Portfolio Value √ó Risk %
   - Position Size = Risk Amount / (Entry Price - Stop Loss Price)

3. **Correlation awareness:**
   - Max 30% in single asset class during risk-on
   - Max 50% in crypto (rest in cash/stocks) during uncertainty
   - Diversify by **strategy** not just assets (social arb + forex + crypto ‚â† correlated)

4. **Drawdown limits:**
   - Stop all new trades if portfolio down 10% from peak
   - Review strategy if down 15%
   - Hard stop / pivot strategy if down 20%

5. **Profit-taking discipline:**
   - Take 20% off table at 2x
   - Take 30% off at 5x
   - Let 50% ride for 10x+
   - **Never regret profits** (greed kills)
