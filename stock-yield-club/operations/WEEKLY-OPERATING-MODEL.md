# Capital Flow Academy - Weekly Operating Model

## Weekly Rhythm Design

### MONDAY - Macro + Positioning (9:00 AM EST)
**Duration:** 60 minutes  
**Format:** Live Zoom call + recording

**Agenda:**
1. **Market Review (15 min)**
   - S&P500/Nasdaq weekly performance
   - Sector rotation analysis
   - VIX / volatility environment
   - Economic calendar (FOMC, CPI, jobs data)

2. **Macro Themes (20 min)**
   - Interest rate outlook
   - Earnings season positioning
   - Geopolitical risks
   - Seasonal patterns (January effect, sell in May, etc.)

3. **Weekly Positioning (15 min)**
   - Bullish/bearish/neutral stance
   - Sectors to overweight/underweight
   - Volatility strategies (high IV = sell premium, low IV = wait)
   - Cash allocation recommendation

4. **Member Q&A (10 min)**
   - Live questions on positioning
   - Portfolio-specific guidance (no individual advice, general frameworks)

**Deliverables:**
- Weekly outlook PDF (2-3 pages)
- Sector heatmap (visual)
- Key levels chart (S&P support/resistance)

---

### TUESDAY - Setup Breakdown (2:00 PM EST)
**Duration:** 45 minutes  
**Format:** Screen share + live trading platform demo

**Agenda:**
1. **Stock Screening (15 min)**
   - Covered call candidates (high IV, quality)
   - Put-selling opportunities (support levels)
   - Earnings plays (upcoming week)
   - Momentum setups (breakouts, trends)

2. **Trade Idea Deep Dive (20 min)**
   - Pick 2-3 specific setups
   - Entry, exit, stop-loss
   - Position sizing example
   - Risk/reward calculation
   - **Disclaimer:** "For educational purposes only, not recommendations"

3. **Technical Analysis (10 min)**
   - Chart review (support/resistance)
   - Indicator signals (RSI, MACD, volume)
   - Pattern recognition (flags, triangles, head & shoulders)

**Deliverables:**
- Setup sheet (3-5 trade ideas)
- Charts with annotations
- Risk calculator spreadsheet

---

### WEDNESDAY - Options Structure (7:00 PM EST)
**Duration:** 60 minutes  
**Format:** Options-focused workshop

**Agenda:**
1. **Options Basics Refresher (10 min)**
   - Greeks quick review (Delta, Theta, Vega, Gamma)
   - IV percentile check (current environment)
   - Expiration calendar (weekly vs monthly)

2. **Income Strategy Workshop (30 min)**
   - Covered call live demo (pick stock, select strike, execute)
   - Cash-secured put walkthrough
   - Wheel strategy in action
   - Rolling techniques (adjusting losing positions)

3. **Advanced Structures (15 min)**
   - Spreads (credit, debit, iron condor)
   - Earnings straddles/strangles
   - Protective puts (portfolio insurance)

4. **Member Trades Review (5 min)**
   - Highlight member wins (with permission)
   - Common mistakes to avoid

**Deliverables:**
- Options playbook PDF
- Strike selection calculator
- Expiration calendar

---

### THURSDAY - Risk Review (11:00 AM EST)
**Duration:** 30 minutes  
**Format:** Dashboard walkthrough

**Agenda:**
1. **Portfolio Health Check (10 min)**
   - Are stops in place?
   - Over-concentrated positions?
   - Correlation check (too many tech stocks?)
   - Margin usage review

2. **Losing Positions Protocol (10 min)**
   - When to cut vs hold
   - Rolling vs closing options
   - Tax-loss harvesting opportunities
   - Emotional discipline check

3. **Hedging Strategies (10 min)**
   - VIX spike protection
   - Put spreads for market insurance
   - Cash allocation (raise cash in frothy markets)

**Deliverables:**
- Risk checklist
- Portfolio analyzer tool
- Hedge calculator

---

### FRIDAY - Portfolio Recap (4:00 PM EST)
**Duration:** 45 minutes  
**Format:** Week-end wrap + community celebration

**Agenda:**
1. **Weekly Performance (15 min)**
   - Market summary (what moved, why)
   - Capital Flow Academy signals performance
   - Member wins spotlight (P&L screenshots, testimonials)

2. **Lessons Learned (15 min)**
   - What worked this week
   - What didn't work
   - Key takeaways for next week

3. **Community Showcase (15 min)**
   - Member questions
   - Strategy discussions
   - Peer learning (experienced members help newbies)

**Deliverables:**
- Weekly recap email
- Signal performance tracker
- Next week preview
