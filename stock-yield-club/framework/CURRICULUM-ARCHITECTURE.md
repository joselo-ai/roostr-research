## 4-Tier Curriculum Ladder

### TIER 1 - FOUNDATIONS (Weeks 1-4)

**Goal:** Safe market entry + capital protection

#### Modules
1. **Market Structure 101**
   - How stocks are priced (bid/ask, spreads)
   - Market vs limit orders
   - Pre-market/after-hours risks
   - Circuit breakers & halts

2. **Stock Basics**
   - Fundamental screening (P/E, ROE, debt ratios)
   - Sector rotation & correlation
   - Dividend aristocrats vs growth
   - Volume & liquidity requirements

3. **Risk Management**
   - Position sizing (1-5% rule)
   - Stop-loss placement
   - Diversification principles
   - Max portfolio risk (10% rule)

4. **Portfolio Construction**
   - Core holdings vs tactical trades
   - Beta management
   - Cash allocation
   - Rebalancing schedules

5. **Broker Setup**
   - Platform comparison (TD, E*TRADE, Schwab, IBKR)
   - Account types (cash vs margin)
   - Tax considerations (wash sales, LTCG)
   - Fees & commissions

6. **Capital Protection Rules**
   - Never risk >2% per trade
   - Emergency exit protocols
   - Black swan hedging basics
   - Emotional discipline frameworks

#### Live Practice
- **Week 1:** Paper trading walkthrough (ThinkorSwim)
- **Week 2:** First simulated stock purchase
- **Week 3:** Stop-loss execution drill
- **Week 4:** Portfolio construction exercise

#### Case Studies
- 2008 Financial Crisis: What broke vs what survived
- 2020 COVID Crash: Recovery patterns
- Individual stock blow-ups: Why stop-losses matter

#### Rules
‚úÖ Complete all 6 modules before Tier 2  
‚úÖ Pass final quiz (80% minimum)  
‚úÖ Submit paper trading journal (10 trades min)  

#### Templates
- Position sizing calculator
- Portfolio allocation spreadsheet
- Trade journal template
- Risk checklist

#### SOPs
- Pre-trade checklist (5 steps before every entry)
- Daily market routine (morning prep)
- Weekly portfolio review

5. **ETF Overlays**
   - SPY/QQQ covered calls

#### Live Practice (Weekly)
- **Week 5-6:** Covered call simulation
