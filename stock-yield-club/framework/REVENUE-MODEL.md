## Churn Management

### Target Churn Rate: <10%/month

**Retention Tactics:**
1. **Onboarding:** 14-day welcome sequence (engagement critical)
2. **Quick wins:** First profitable trade within 30 days
3. **Community:** Active Discord = 50% lower churn
4. **Certification:** Gamification keeps members engaged
5. **Results tracking:** Dashboard shows progress = sticky

**Churn Scenarios:**
- **Month 1:** 20% churn (didn't engage)
- **Month 2-3:** 10% churn (exploring, not committed)
- **Month 4-12:** 5% churn (sticky, seeing results)
- **12+ months:** 3% churn (evangelists)

**Net Retention:** 88% (after 12 months)

## Pricing Psychology

### Why 3 Tiers?
- **Anchoring:** Tier 3 makes Tier 2 look reasonable
- **Choice:** People buy more when given 3 options vs 2
- **Upsell Path:** Easy to upgrade Tier 1 → 2 → 3

### Annual Discount (2 months free)
- **Cash flow boost:** Upfront revenue
- **Lower churn:** 12-month commitment = less likely to quit
- **Target:** 30% of members choose annual

### Price Increases
- **Founding member lock:** Early adopters keep $197/$497/$997 forever
- **New members:** Increase by $50-$100 every 6 months
- **Value justification:** More tools, proven results, larger community
