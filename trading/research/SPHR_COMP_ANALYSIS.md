## RISK FACTORS

### Investment Risks (Ranked by Severity)

1. **Single-Asset Concentration (Critical)**
   - 100% revenue from Las Vegas Sphere
   - Technology failure, fire, or operational disruption = total revenue loss
   - **Mitigation:** Insurance, operational redundancies, accelerate expansion

2. **Unproven Global Expansion (High)**
   - No evidence Sphere concept works outside Las Vegas
   - International markets may have different consumer preferences
   - **Mitigation:** Pilot in proven market (London), thorough market research

3. **Event-Driven Revenue Volatility (High)**
   - Dependent on artist residency bookings
   - No recurring revenue model
   - **Mitigation:** Diversify revenue (corporate events, Exosphere ads, screenings)

4. **Capex Intensity (Medium)**
   - $2B+ per new venue limits scale speed
   - Capital allocation risk if expansion fails
   - **Mitigation:** JVs, partnerships, asset-light models

5. **Macro Sensitivity (Medium)**
   - Consumer discretionary spending downturn
   - Las Vegas tourism decline
   - **Mitigation:** Premium positioning, unique experiences (recession-resistant?)

6. **Technology Commoditization (Low-Medium)**
   - Immersive tech becomes accessible to competitors
   - Samsung, Sony could license similar displays
   - **Mitigation:** First-mover brand, venue design IP, content partnerships
