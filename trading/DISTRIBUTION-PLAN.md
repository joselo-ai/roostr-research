## Crisis Management

### If Growth Stalls (Week 4-6)
**Diagnosis:**
1. Check analytics (what's not working?)
2. Review competitor content (what are they doing differently?)
3. Survey audience (ask what they want to see)

**Pivots to try:**
- Increase video frequency (2x/day on TikTok)
- Change content mix (more education, less updates)
- Test new formats (live streams, collaborations, stitches)
- Go controversial (hot takes, scam callouts)

### If Negative Feedback Spikes
**Response protocol:**
1. Acknowledge with data (show transparency)
2. If valid criticism, commit to change
3. If trolling/bots, ignore (don't feed)
4. Turn into content ("Someone said X, here's why they're wrong")

### If Account Suspension/Shadowban
**Prevention:**
- Avoid spam-like behavior (max 3 hashtags on X, 5 on TikTok)
- Don't buy followers (ever)
- Diversify platforms (don't rely on one)

**Response:**
- Appeal immediately (all platforms have processes)
- Shift focus to other platforms temporarily
- Notify audience via available channels
