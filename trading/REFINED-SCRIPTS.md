## Production Notes

### Video Format Specs
**Resolution:** 1080x1920 (vertical) or 1920x1080 (horizontal)  
**Aspect ratios:**
- TikTok: 9:16 (vertical)
- YouTube Shorts: 9:16 (vertical)
- X/Twitter: 16:9 or 1:1 (square)
- LinkedIn: 16:9 (horizontal)

**Length targets:**
- 30s videos: 70-80 words
- 45s videos: 95-110 words
- 60s videos: 125-140 words

**Pacing:** ~2.5 words/second (moderate pace, clear delivery)

### Visual Guidelines
**Background elements:**
- Trading dashboard (dark theme)
- Live charts (relevant to topic)
- RSI/technical indicators (when mentioned)
- Minimal text overlays (3-5 words max)

**Avatar placement:**
- Lower-right or lower-left corner (doesn't block charts)
- Subtle animated gestures
- Expressive face for emphasis

**Branding:**
- "roostr Capital" logo (top-left, small)
- "$100K â†’ $X" tracker (top-right, updates weekly)
- End card: X handle + GitHub link (2 seconds)

### Voice/Tone
**Delivery:**
- Confident, not arrogant
- Clear enunciation
- Pauses for emphasis (after key points)
- Natural pacing (not rushed)

**Personality:**
- Direct, no fluff
- Educational but not condescending
- Transparent about losses
- Analytical, data-driven

### Hooks (First 3 Seconds)
Every video must have a compelling hook:
- Pattern: "Down X% on Y. Here's why..."
- Pattern: "We just built X in Y minutes..."
- Pattern: "Most traders do X. We do Y."
- Pattern: "Here's what happened..."

**Goal:** Stop the scroll, promise value

### Call-to-Action (Last 3 Seconds)
Every video ends with:
- Short tagline: "roostr Capital" or "Building in public"
- Optional CTA: "Follow along" / "See more on GitHub" / "Day X complete"
